 University of Northern British Columbia 
 北 英属 哥伦比亚 大学 
 The University of Northern British Columbia is one of Canada ’ s newest public universities and has already achieved an excellent domestic and international reputation . In 2009 , UNBC was ranked as the best small university in Western Canada and # 2 out of 21 small universities in Canada by Maclean ’ s Magazine 。 
 UNBC aspires to be Canada ’ s premier small , research-intensive university , with internationally recognized academic and research programs that prepare its graduates in select areas of high relevance to our region , province , and be yond . With a core campus in Prince George and regional campuses throughout northern BC , UNBC recognizes the importance of quality teaching , personal attention , and ground - breaking research 。 
 Students seeking to meet UNBC English Language proficiency requirements for admission in to Undergraduate program may do so through the English Language Studies program .
