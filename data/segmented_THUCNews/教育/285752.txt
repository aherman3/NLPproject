 新布伦瑞克 大学 
 Established in 1785 , the University of New Brunswick (UN B) provides undergraduate and graduate degree programs on two campuses in the cities of Fredericton and Saint John 。 
 As the oldest English university in Canada , UN B has a proud history of educational excellence , at tracting top students from over 100 countries each year . The largest university in the province of New Brunswick , UN B is , by reputation , one of the top five universities in Canada . Students may take advantage of our exciting co - op programs and Employment Centres to gain valuable Canadian work experience . UN B's degree programs , offering over 150 different specialties under 13 different faculties , are innovative and progressive . UN B offers dynamic degrees in the following subject areas : Arts , Business Administration , Science , Computer Science , Engineering , Education , Nursing , Environmental Studies , Forestry , Law , Heal th Sciences , and Sports Science . Students may be conditionally admitted to some undergraduate programs without submitting an English language test score . Conditionally accepted students are assessed on the ir English language skills when they arrive on campus .
