教育展 参展 高校 简介 ： 德国 弗赖 贝格 工业 大学 
 2009年 中国 国际 教育展 将 于 10月 17日 在 北京 拉开 帷幕 ， 随后 巡回 武汉 、 上海 、 成都 和 广州 5 个 城市 ， 以下 为 德国 此次 参展 院校 德国 弗赖 贝格 工业 大学 简介 ： 
 The Bergakademie Freiberg was founded in 1765 and is the world ’ s oldest school of mining and metallurgy . Several centuries of successful silver mining preceded its foundation 。 
 Still today , teaching and research at our university are directed towards the need of industry . Practical experience was and is today a hall mark of education in Freiberg 。 
 The mission of our Technical University has always been to research and produce raw materials and energy in consideration of ecological balance . The four core competencies include the environment , materials , geo sciences and energy 。 
 TU Bergakademie Freiberg has about 5 , 000 students and offers all the advantages of a small university : short distances , no overcrowded lecture halls , an excellent ratio of students to professors , rapid access to literature , to laboratories and to training positions as well as a familiar atmosphere at the university and on campus 。 
 TU Bergakademie Freiberg is very closely connected with the city of Freiberg and its 45 , 000 inhabitants . Founded more than 800 years a go , Freiberg still today strongly reflects this mediaeval charm 。
