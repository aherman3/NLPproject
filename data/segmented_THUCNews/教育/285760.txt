 Wageningen University -- University of life sciences , food and environment 
 Wageningen University and Research Center (Wageningen UR ) is a unique organisation that combines fundamental , strategic and applied research with innovative education at BSc , MSc and PhD levels . During recent decades Wageningen University has evolved in to one of the world 's leading centres and even number one in Europe in life sciences , food and environment . Its research and education are both international and internationally renowned , which at tracts students from more than 100 countries all over the world 。 
 Wageningen University is listed in the top 100 of all universities in the world and the language of instruction is English . Students and guests find in Wageningen a pleasant study environment and an opportunity for exchange of expertise .
