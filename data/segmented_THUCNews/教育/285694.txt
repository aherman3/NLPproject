教育展 参展 高校 简介 ： 一 桥 大学 
 2009年 中国 国际 教育展 将 于 10月 17日 在 北京 拉开 帷幕 ， 随后 巡回 武汉 、 上海 、 成都 和 广州 5 个 城市 ， 以下 为 日本国 此次 参展 院校 一 桥 大学 简介 ： 
 Hitot subashi University 
 一 桥 大学 
 Hitot subashi University is an academic institution that primarily focuses on the social sciences and the study of civil society . The university has been contributing to the development of culture as well as the social economy in Japan with a traditionally liberal,lively academic climate . 
 Since the university 's foundation , faculty and staff alike have endeavored to enhance standards of education and research . Hitot subashi University has also been producing prominent individuals active in business and social circles in Japan and abroad , which we are proud of .
