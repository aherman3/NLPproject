 TU9 Ger man Institutes of Technology : RWTH Aachen , TU Berlin , TU Braunschweig , TU Darmstadt , TU Dresden , Universität Hannover , Karlsruhe Institute of Technology (KIT) ， TU München , Universität Stuttgart 
 TU9 is the network of the 9 leading technical universities in Ger many . The ir educational and research focus is on engineering and the sciences , areas in which they cooperate closely with national and international industries . TU9 universities are centres of scientific and technical innovation . They are linked with the most important national and international research institutions which maintain branch labs on or in the vicinity of the ir campuses 。 
 TU9 universities are partners of prestigious institutes of higher education all over the world with which they have agreements on the mutual recognition of courses , credits and degrees in order to facilitate the international mobility of students and graduates .
